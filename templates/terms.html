Schoology Recap Terms

Presented by the Pinewood Tech Club

Schoology Recap is a really basic project built by students for students. As such, we treat your data with the utmost care and respect.

By generating your recap, you agree that we will use your data to:

  • Process assignment data to generate your recap
  • Email you when your recap is ready and for other Tech Club news
  • To create anonymous aggregate statistics about student behavior
  • To record anonymous telemetry data to improve our product

The Pinewood Tech club will not:

  • Store any of your Schoology data other than the generated statistics that are displayed in your recap
  • Sell or share your data with any third parties beyond the services that we use to provide our service (listed below)
  • Access data that is not relevant to the service (we access submission time only, not content)

In order to run our service, we rely on select third-party services for certain functionality. Data sent to these services are subject to their respective terms of service and privacy policies.

  • Amazon Web Services for hosting and email delivery. AWS receives: your email address, assignment metadata (submission times, course names, grades), and generated recap statistics. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare for DNS services. Cloudflare may receive your IP address during DNS lookups. Privacy Policy
  • Schoology for accessing your assignment data via their API. Privacy Policy

If you have any questions or concerns about our terms, please contact us at techclub@pinewood.edu. We are always happy to answer any clarification questions. Thank you for using Schoology Recap!
